# Financial Analysis of boAt (Imagine Marketing Pvt. Ltd.)

## Project Overview
This project is a final year academic study on the financial analysis of boAt (Imagine Marketing Pvt. Ltd.), a private limited company in the consumer electronics and audio accessories industry.

The analysis evaluates the financial performance and position of the company using financial statements and ratio analysis.

## Period of Study
2020 – 2024

## Objectives of the Study
- To analyze the financial performance of the company
- To evaluate liquidity, profitability, and solvency
- To understand the financial position through ratio analysis
- To interpret financial data using charts and graphs

## Methodology
- Analysis of financial statements
- Ratio analysis
- Graphical representation using charts and bar diagrams

## Tools Used
- Financial Statement Analysis
- Ratio Analysis
- MS Excel (for charts and calculations)
- Academic financial data sources

## Key Insights
- Financial trends over the study period were analyzed
- Strengths and weaknesses of the company were identified
- Ratios were used to assess financial stability and performance

## Conclusion
Based on the financial and ratio analysis, conclusions were drawn regarding the financial health and performance of boAt (Imagine Marketing Pvt. Ltd.) during the period 2020–2024.
